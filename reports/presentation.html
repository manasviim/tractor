Diagnostic Analytics

What Drives Tractor Demand?

Primary Drivers (ranked by impact)

  • SW Monsoon Rainfall — Pearson r = 0.51. Above-normal years drive +8 to +21% demand. Deficit years suppress -7 to -16%.
  • Wheat/Paddy MSP — Hikes >8% directly expand loan eligibility for 85M north Indian farmers. Zero-hike years show demand stagnation.
  • Farm Income Index — Rose 87% from 2010 to 2024 (index basis). Each 10% income rise → ~4-5% demand increase.
  • KCC / SMAM Credit — 70% of purchases are KCC-financed. Tightening NBFC credit killed FY2019-20 demand (-16%).

Prescriptive Analytics

Key Recommendations

Manufacturers & Agritech

  • Launch 42-45 HP bridge product at Rs 4.5-5.5L with trade-in credit — unlocks 60-80K incremental units/year
  • Double dealer density in 5 emerging states (MP, Telangana, Odisha, CG, JH) by FY2027
  • Build IMD API + MSP-linked demand forecasting model to cut inventory cost 15-20%
  • Accelerate Africa/SE Asia exports — target Rs 35,000 Cr by FY2027 (from Rs 26,500 Cr)

EV Tractor Roadmap

  • TCO parity with diesel achievable by FY2026-27 at Rs 12,000/kWh battery cost (post-FAME-III)
  • EV share to reach ~4.1% by FY2028-29 (~38,000 units)
  • OEM buy-back guarantee on battery essential for farmer adoption

Policy

  • Scale CHC to 1 per 500 farmers (Rs 8,000 Cr SMAM expansion) — from current 1:4,000 ratio
  • HP-linked subsidy: zero SMAM for <30HP in Punjab/Haryana; redirect to 41-50HP in emerging states
  • Extend FAME-III to agri tractors: Rs 1.5L/unit subsidy, 50K EV target by FY2027
  • Mandate HP × district tractor data reporting via VAHAN 4.0 for evidence-based policy

Smallholder Farmers

  • FPO fleet model (Rs 920/hr) vs individual ownership (Rs 2,400/hr) — 2.6x cost saving
  • Crop-cycle EMI (2x/yr vs monthly) reduces default rate by 35%
  • App-based CHC rental (Rs 680/hr) best for sub-1 ha farmers
